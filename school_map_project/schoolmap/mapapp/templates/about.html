About FINDIT

Your Campus Navigation and Information System

System Overview

FINDIT is a campus navigation and information system designed to help students and teachers locate classrooms, buildings, and facilities within the school.

It also provides easy access to schedules, user profiles, and updates ‚Äî all in one interactive platform.

Mission and Vision

Mission:

To simplify school navigation and promote digital efficiency for both students and staff.

Vision:

To become the most reliable and user-friendly school mapping system for every campus.

Key Features

Interactive Campus Map

Navigate through the school with our easy-to-use map interface.

Student & Teacher Profiles

View and manage user profiles with relevant information.

Class Schedules

Access and manage class schedules in one place.

Customizable Themes

Choose from Light, Dark, and Sunset themes for better visibility.

Quick Search

Find locations quickly with our powerful search functionality.

Notifications

Stay updated with important announcements and alerts.

Who Can Use FINDIT

Students

Find your classrooms and teachers easily.

Teachers

Manage your class schedules and room assignments.

Visitors

Locate buildings or offices on campus.

Administrators

Update and manage campus data efficiently.
