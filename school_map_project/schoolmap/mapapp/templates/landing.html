Your Guide to Everything Academic

What is CampusNavigator?

CampusNavigator is the essential mobile and web platform designed to streamline your daily life on campus. Whether you're a student rushing to class, a faculty member needing to connect, or a visitor trying to find your way, our system puts all the information you need right at your fingertips.

We replace bulky paper schedules and confusing campus signs with a unified, instant digital experience, saving you time and reducing stress.

Key Benefits & Features

CampusNavigator is built to make your academic journey simpler and more organized.

Interactive Campus Map

A dynamic, searchable map of all campus buildings, departments, and points of interest.

Never get lost finding your next lecture or office.

Building Search & Details

Look up specific buildings to see floor plans, room numbers, and facility info.

Find your destination quickly within any building.

Personal Schedule

View your current, weekly, and future class schedules in a clean, calendar-style format.

Stay on top of your classes without multiple sources.

Our Mission

To foster a more connected, efficient, and navigable campus community by providing powerful, user-friendly digital tools that empower every member to succeed.
